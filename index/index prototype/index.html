Exercise 1: Self Portrait

Hi guys, I'm Cathy!

I'm going into my 4th year of Graphic Design.

I chose to use p5.js (Links to an external site.) as my website tool to create this self portrait of me and my cat, Koi. I got my cat last year during the pandemic and honestly was one of the best decision I've ever made. I actually incorporated her into two of my other projects last term in Graphic Design 2, so she's been most of my inspiration so far. I've been trying to improve my coding knowledge and skills so what better way to do that than coding my own picture.

Other than that I've been trying to get back into drawing and improving how to use procreate. I really like making DIYS and handcrafts like using polymer clay. I've been seeing artisan keycaps in my Instagram feed a lot and I wanna try making my own.
